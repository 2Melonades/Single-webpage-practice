The Gospel explained

Are you aware that being right with God is a gift?

Do you know that righteousness with God is not what you can earn through your performance or actions?
Have you seen these scriptures?


Romans 6:32 "For the wages of sin is death, but the free gift of God is eternal life through Christ Jesus our Lord"

Romans 4:5 (NLT) "But people are counted as righteous, not because of their faith in God who forgives sinners"

Galatians 2:21 "I do not treat the grace of God as meaningless. For if keeping the law could make us right with God, then there was no need for Christ to die."

Are you also aware that this gift is everlasting?

Romans 4:8 "Blessed is the one whose sin the Lord will never count against them"

Are you aware that all the sins of your entire life have been paid for and cancelled by God? (1 John 2:2)

Corinthians 5:21 "For God made Christ, who never sinned, to be the offspring for our sin so that we could be made right with God through Christ"

2 Corinthians 5:19 "For God was in Christ, reconciling the world to himself, no longer counting people's sins against. He gave us this wonderful message of reconciliation."

John 1:29 "... Behold! the Lamb of God who takes away the sin of the world!"

Do you know that going to Heaven is not dependent on how good you are but solely on what Jesus has done for you

Epheshians 2:8-9 "God saved you by his grace when you believed, and you can't take credit for this; it is a gift from God. Salvation is not a reward for the good things we have done, so none of us can boast about it."

Romans 10:9 "If ou decllare with your mouth "Jesus is Lord" and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved."

I would like you to know that there is something precious that Jesus Christ the Son of God purchased for you by His sacrifice over 2000 years ago; an inheritance was given to you from Jesus and I will like to tell you more about it; how to receive it, and how to use it.
